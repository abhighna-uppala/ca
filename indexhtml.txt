    <h2>Cloud Computing</h2>
    <p>Cloud computing is the delivery of various services through the Internet. These resources include tools and applications like data storage, servers, databases, networking, and software. Cloud-based solutions can provide advantages such as scalability, flexibility, cost-effectiveness, and accessibility.</p>
    <p>Organizations and individuals can use cloud computing services to store and process data, run applications, host websites, and more, without having to manage physical infrastructure.</p>
    <p>Key benefits of cloud computing include:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Scalability and flexibility: Resources can be scaled up or down based on demand.</li>
      <li>Cost-effectiveness: Pay only for the resources and services used.</li>
      <li>Accessibility: Access data and applications from anywhere with an internet connection.</li>
      <li>Reliability and security: Cloud providers often have robust security measures and redundant systems.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>Overall, cloud computing has revolutionized how businesses and individuals use and manage technology, offering new opportunities for innovation and efficiency.</p>
